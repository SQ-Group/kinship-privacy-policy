Privacy Policy

Kinship is a public-facing app by Q Collection with optional employee sign‑in. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Introduction

At Kinship (by Q Collection), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Kinship app.

Scope & Audience

Public Users

Anyone can access the following modules without providing any personal information:

  • About Us
  • Business
  • Offices
  • News
  • Gallery

Employees

Employees can sign in to access internal modules from the sidebar. Employee‑only features require limited personal data for authentication and functionality.

Information We Collect

Public Users

We do not require personal information to access public modules. Technical logs (e.g., device and performance data) may be collected to maintain and improve the app.

Employees (upon login)

  • Name – to personalize your profile and app experience.
  • Email Address – for authentication and secure access.
  • Photo – for profile display and internal identification.
  • Camera Access – for capturing images or videos only when a feature requires it.
  • Audio Access – for features requiring voice input or recordings.

We collect only what is necessary to provide the requested functionality.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve app features and services.
  • Enable secure login and access to employee‑only modules.
  • Personalize the employee experience within the app.
  • Communicate internal updates where applicable.
  • Analyze usage to enhance performance and reliability.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

Public users’ personal data is not collected unless they voluntarily contact us through external channels (e.g., email).

App Permissions

  • Camera – used only when you choose a feature that requires capturing photos or videos.
  • Photos/Media – used to upload a profile picture or share internal content where applicable.
  • Audio – used for internal features requiring voice input or recordings.

You can disable these permissions at any time in your device settings, though some features may not work without them.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry‑standard security measures such as encryption, secure storage, and access controls to safeguard data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only to comply with the law, protect our rights, or maintain app security.

Your Data Rights

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to applicable company or legal requirements.
  • Manage permissions (camera, photos, audio) in your device settings.

Depending on your region (e.g., the European Union), you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws.

Cookies & Tracking

Kinship does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies for public access to the app’s open modules. Limited technical logs may be collected to monitor performance and troubleshoot issues.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in features, practices, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted in‑app, and material changes will be clearly communicated.
