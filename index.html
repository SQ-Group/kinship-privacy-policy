Privacy Policy

Kinship is a public-facing mobile application developed by Q Collection. The application provides general information globally, while authenticated users in regions such as the UK, Singapore, Morocco, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka are granted access to additional features, including communications, grievance management, organizational services, and survey participation. This policy outlines the types of information we collect, the purposes for which it is used, and the measures in place to safeguard your rights and privacy.

At Kinship (by Q Collection), we respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. We collect and use limited data to provide services such as communication, grievance management, and surveys, while ensuring your information is protected. By using Kinship, you agree to this policy and your rights remain fully protected.

Public Users

The following modules are accessible to all users without requiring any personal information:

  • About Us
  • Business
  • Offices
  • News
  • Gallery

No personal information is required to access these public modules. However, the app may collect basic technical data (such as device and performance logs) to ensure app functionality, security, and continuous improvement.

Authenticated Users

Registered and verified users can sign in to access internal modules via the sidebar. Certain employee-only features may require limited personal information strictly to enable authentication and ensure proper functionality.

Employees (Upon Login)

For authenticated employees, limited personal information is collected strictly for authentication and functionality purposes:

  • Name – to personalize your profile and enhance app experience.
  • Email Address – to enable secure login and access.
  • Photo – for profile display and internal identification.

Permissions (only when a feature requires them):

  • Camera Access – only when a feature requires capturing images or videos.
  • Audio Access – only for features that involve voice input or recordings.

We collect only what is necessary to provide the requested functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and enhance app features and services.
  • To enable secure login and access to employee-only modules.
  • To personalize the employee experience within the app.
  • To communicate internal updates, where applicable.
  • To analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and reliability.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

For public users, personal data is not collected unless voluntarily provided through external channels, such as email.

Your Rights & Security

At Kinship, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to safeguard data. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

You have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. Employees may update their details within the app or contact support for assistance. Depending on your region (for example, the European Union), additional rights may apply under local data protection laws.
